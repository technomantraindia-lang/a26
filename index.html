About Us
  • History
  • Mission
  • Vision

Established in 2016 by Apurva Suthar and Unnati Suthar, A26 DESIGNS has grown as an architecture and interior design studio focused on residential architecture, luxury interiors and complete turnkey execution.

From our studio in Himatnagar, we bring thoughtful planning, refined detailing and dependable site coordination together to create homes that feel timeless, functional and personal.

Our mission is to design spaces that seamlessly blend aesthetics, functionality and comfort while delivering every project with precision, transparency and uncompromising quality.

We provide end-to-end architecture, interior design and turnkey execution, helping clients move from concept to completion with clear communication and careful attention to every detail.

Our vision is to become a leading architecture and interior design studio known for creating timeless, innovative and functional spaces that inspire better living.

We aim to set new benchmarks in design excellence through creativity, quality craftsmanship, sustainable thinking and client-focused solutions that reflect each lifestyle beautifully.

Design. Build. Belong.

From the first concept to final execution, we create spaces tailored to the way you live and work.
