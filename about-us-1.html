About Us

Since 2016 Our Story

Designing Spaces.
Crafting Experiences.

At A26 DESIGNS, exceptional spaces are created through thoughtful design, attention to detail and a deep understanding of the people who live in them.

Established in 2016 by Apurva Suthar and Unnati Suthar, A26 DESIGNS is an architecture and interior design studio specializing in residential architecture, luxury interiors and turnkey design execution.

From concept development and space planning to execution and project management, we provide end-to-end design solutions that make every project seamless from start to finish.

  • Luxury Interior Design
